Privacy Policy

At NT Estate Partners, we respect your privacy and protect your information with the highest standards of care.

Data Collection:

  • We collect names, email addresses, and project details exclusively for referral matching purposes.
  • All information is used to connect qualified developers with vetted contractors and vice versa.
  • We do not collect unnecessary personal information beyond what is required for partnership facilitation.

Data Usage:

  • Your information is used solely to identify suitable partnership opportunities.
  • We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • Communications regarding potential matches are handled confidentially within our operations team.

Data Protection:

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely on private, authenticated servers.
  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized NT Estate Partners personnel only.
  • We never share your contact information without explicit consent.

For inquiries regarding your data or privacy concerns, contact us directly at ops@ntestatepartners.com.

Referral Terms & Commission Structure

Commission Rate:

  • Standard referral commission: 3% of total project contract value.
  • Commissions are only earned on Qualified Sales.
  • No commissions are earned on projects below the minimum threshold.

Qualified Sales Definition:

  • A Qualified Sale is defined as any project contract that explicitly results from an NT Estate Partners referral.
  • The project contract value must exceed $500,000 USD (or equivalent in other currencies).
  • The contractor must have been introduced through NT Estate Partners' network.
  • The project must be executed within 180 days of the referral date to qualify for commission.

Commission Payout Terms:

  • Commissions are calculated upon contract execution between the developer and contractor.
  • Payment is issued within 30 days of the contractor receiving initial project funds.
  • Payouts are made via direct deposit to the account specified by the referring partner.
  • NT Estate Partners does not issue payments for incomplete or disputed projects.

Commission Disputes:

  • Any disputes regarding commission eligibility must be raised within 14 days of project initiation.
  • NT Estate Partners will investigate and provide a written determination within 10 business days.
  • All determinations are final and binding.
